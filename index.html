Ukraine QA Association

Uniting QA Professionals Across Ukraine

We bring together manual and automation QA engineers to share experience, collaborate, and grow professionally.

Our Mission

The Ukraine QA Association is a professional community of quality assurance specialists. Our mission is to support each other, organize knowledge-sharing events, and promote professional standards in QA across the country.

Why It Matters

Quality assurance plays a critical role in building reliable software products. By joining the Ukraine QA Association, you become part of a strong network where you can exchange knowledge, attend events and webinars, and contribute to the national QA ecosystem.

Membership Benefits

  • Private Telegram group: Connect with experienced QA professionals, ask questions, and share knowledge.
  • Exclusive learning resources: Access to courses, recorded webinars, and curated testing materials.
  • Hands-on workshops: Live sessions on automation tools, best practices, and real project examples.
  • Monthly expert Q&As: Interact with senior QA engineers and test architects in live discussions.
  • Job board and freelance leads: Internal listings from trusted employers and community members.
  • QA hackathons and bug hunts: Participate in competitions to sharpen your skills and win recognition.
  • Mentorship program: Get paired with an experienced mentor to accelerate your growth.
  • Certificate of membership: A digital badge that confirms your professional affiliation with the association.
  • Publishing and speaking opportunities: Share your knowledge through talks and articles within the community.
  • Networking and local meetups: Build meaningful connections and collaborate with peers nationwide.

How to Join

To become a member, applicants must meet the following criteria:

  • Minimum 5 years of experience in QA (manual or automation)
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science
  • Demonstrate extraordinary achievements in work or research within the field
  • Strong technical and analytical skills
